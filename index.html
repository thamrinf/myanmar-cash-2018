Myanmar Cash Transfer Programming activities in 2018

Reset filters

Cash Transfer Programming (CTP) interventions have increasingly been used by humanitarian organisations in Myanmar since Cyclone Nargis in 2008. In 2018, humanitarian organisations reached more than 612,000 individuals with cash and humanitarian cash transfers totaling more than $13.8 million. Cash Transfer Programming is increasingly part of preparedness efforts. Innovations such as mobile money provide opportunities for further scale up CTP in Myanmar.
